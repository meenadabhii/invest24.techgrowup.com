Saving Money

Learn how to save money effectively and develop healthy financial habits that will last a lifetime.

Why Save Money?

Saving money helps you prepare for emergencies, reach your goals, and build financial security for the future.

How to Start Saving

Start small by setting aside a portion of your allowance or earnings. Even saving just 10% can make a big difference over time.

Setting Savings Goals

Set specific, achievable goals for what you want to save for, like a new video game, smartphone, or college fund.

Budgeting Basics

Learn how to create and stick to a budget to make the most of your money.

What is a Budget?

A budget is a plan for how you will spend your money. It helps you prioritize your spending and avoid running out of money.

Creating Your First Budget

List your income (allowance, gifts, earnings) and expenses (snacks, entertainment, savings) to create a simple budget.
